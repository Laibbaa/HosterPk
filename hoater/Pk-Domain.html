About .PK Domain Names
Discover which .pk extension suits best for your online presence:

PK domains are registered using Pakistan’s country code top-level domain (ccTLD), which is “.pk.” A .pk domain is a web address that ends in “.pk” and is intended for use by individuals or organizations in Pakistan.

You can register the following domain extensions under the .pk ccTLD:

  • .com.pk – General Business, or Individual
  • .pk – Same as .com.pk but without .com
  • .net.pk – Network or IT Related Business
  • .org.pk – Non-profit Organizations
  • .edu.pk – Educational Institutes
  • .fam.pk – Family and Individuals
  • .biz.pk – General Business, Promotional
  • .gov.pk – for Domains for Government of Pakistan
  • .gob.pk – Domains for Government of Baluchistan
  • .gkp.pk – Domains for Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa
  • .gog.pk – Domains for Government of Gilgit Baltistan
  • .gop.pk – Domains for Government of Punjab
  • .gos.pk – Domains for Government of Sindh
  • .gok.pk – Domains for Government of Azad Kashmir

PK domain names are managed and maintained by the PKNIC (Pakistan Network Information Center), the organization responsible for administering the .pk ccTLD. PKNIC was established in 1992 by the Pakistan government, headquartered in Lahore.

Local vs Intl. Domains
.pk vs. .com – Benefits of Having .pk Domain Over a .com
Country code top-level domains (ccTLDs), such as .pk domains for Pakistan, are domain names that are specific to a country and tend to be more desirable, e.g., following are a few reasons why you should choose a .pk domain over other generic top-level domains (such as .com, .net, etc.):

Local relevance:

.pk domains can help establish a local online presence and make your website more relevant to country-specific users. For example, a website with a “.fr” ccTLD (France) is likely to be more relevant to users in France than a website with a “.com” gTLD.

SEO Benefits:

Search engines often give preference to websites with local TLDs, so a PK domain can help increase your visibility to users in your target market, resulting in your website’s ranking improvements for search results in Pakistan.

Credibility:

.pk can add credibility to your website and give the impression that the website is more established and trustworthy e.g. a PK domain can help establish your website as a legitimate presence in Pakistan and give users confidence in your business or organization.

More Chance of Domain Availability:

Since it is tough to get a good domain name .com etc. A .pk domain can make it easier for you to get the domain of your choice.

However, it is worth noting that a .pk domain on its own is sometimes better than gTLDs, and the domain choice will depend on your specific needs and goals. e.g., .pk domains are often used along with generic top-level domains (gTLDs) such as “.com” and “.net.” For example, a Pakistani business might register a gTLD such as “example.com” as well as example.pk. This allows the business to reach a wider audience, provide users with multiple ways to access its website, and have branding control.

Here is a quick guide and definitions so you can choose the best domain extension based on your circumstances:

  • .com, .info, .biz, .org, .net, .name are Global Top Level Domains (or gTLD for short). These do not have much affect on a website’s rankings in search engines (SEO), but users are more familiar with .com,.net, .org domains and if your intent is to service global audience .com is your best choice.
  • .pk, .com.pk etc are ‘Country Code Top Level Domains’ (or ccTLD for short) but .PK domains significantly affect the ranking in search engines thus improves SEO of a site among the national search results.

Here are the valid requirements for .pk and .com.pk ccTLDs:

  • .pk :- For .pk domain minimum of 4 characters are required. So domains like abc.pk, 123.pk etc are not supported, a maximum of 67 characters are supported for all .pk domains
  • .com.pk etc:-All other domains with .pk extension like .com.pk, .net.pk etc can be registered with even one character like 1.com.pk etc.
